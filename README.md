## ⚡ About

AI Forward Deployed Engineer focused on building **production-grade agentic systems** — from local LLM pipelines to national-scale intelligence infrastructure. My work sits at the intersection of AI orchestration, OSINT tooling, and real-world deployment.

Currently engineering **agentic AI infrastructure for BNPT** (Indonesian National Counter-Terrorism Agency) — a national-scale multi-agent system built for intelligence operations.

Background in E-Commerce AI, behavioral algorithm analysis, and signal-based systems thinking. I don't just wire APIs — I build systems that reason.
